Meet the CEO

As a sole proprietorship, the company is currently managed by Edward Osei Bonsu. He is a Trainee Structural Engineer with GNPC-Technip Engineering Services Limited. He Completed Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology with Honors in BSc. Mechanical Engineering. While on campus, he held several leadership positions. These included, the Hall Overseer for PENSA-KNUST and Fundraising Committee Chairman to mention a few.

Growing up, he has been in charge of his father’s shop making him very good and critical about records keeping and being diligent in business dealings. He aspires to become a well-established entrepreneur with a good number of thriving businesses. He looks forward to hiring, as and when needed, people who share similar passion to aid in the administration of his ventures.
