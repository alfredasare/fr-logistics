About Us

We’re a group of meticulous individuals whose
sole purpose is to see your logistics moved
and delivered to your satisfaction

FR Logistics is a trucking and warehousing company that provides its clientele safe, affordable and reliable delivery and warehousing services to their specifications. Our customers and potential customers alike can be rest assured that they will get quality services at competitive rates. We go the extra mile to ensure the safety of goods under our care and that our customers get value for their money. At FR logistics, our goal is to provide excellent, fast and reliable logistics services to our customers.

FR logistics provides the services of safe keeping and transporting items of individuals, students and small scale businesses in Greater Accra, Ashanti and Eastern region to and from the stated regions. Luggage collection is done at the customer’s doorstep with time update on the truck location with delivery carried out to the customer’s satisfaction.

This service will be rendered at very affordable prices where pricing will depend on the number, size and weight, and how delicate the items are. FR logistics is willing to pay up to half the price of an item or forfeit the service charge depending on the extent of damage on the item of a client.

Meet the CEO

As a sole proprietorship, the company is currently managed by Edward Osei Bonsu. He is a Trainee Structural Engineer with GNPC-Technip Engineering Services Limited. He Completed Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology with Honors in BSc. Mechanical Engineering. While on campus, he held several leadership positions. These included, the University Hall Overseer and Fundraising Committee Chairman for PENSA-KNUST to mention a few.

Growing up, he has been in charge of his father’s shop making him very good and critical about records keeping and being diligent in business dealings. He aspires to become a well-established entrepreneur with a good number of thriving businesses. He looks forward to hiring, as and when needed, people who share similar passion to aid in the administration of his ventures.
